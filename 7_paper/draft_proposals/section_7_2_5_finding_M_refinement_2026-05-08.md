## Source 의 actual substantive 영역

### Pierce-Schott 2020 (pierce2020.md)

- Abstract (line 7): outcome 은 "fatal drug overdoses, specifically among whites"; mechanism 은 "deterioration in labor market conditions and uptake of disability insurance"
- Line 31: "the link between PNTR and mortality is driven by drug overdoses... we find little relationship between PNTR and mortality from either suicide or alcohol-related liver disease"
- Line 221: "These results suggest a labor market mechanism"
- Line 225: prescription opioid 는 robustness control variable (state-level opioid laws) 영역에 등장
- **Pierce-Schott 2020 의 substantive direction**: drug overdose 가 outcome, labor market 이 mechanism. opioid 는 robustness control 영역, primary causal mediator 로 specify 안 됨.

### Autor-Dorn-Hanson 2019 (autor2019.md)

- Abstract + paper title: main outcomes 는 marriage, fertility, children's living circumstances; mortality 는 supporting evidence
- Line 231: "D&A deaths" point estimate 19.5 per 100,000 per decade (drug and alcohol 통합)
- "Prescription opioids" 라는 표현 paper 안에 등장 안 함
- **ADH 2019 의 substantive direction**: Becker/Wilson framework (marriage market value of young men decline). Mechanism 은 marriage market.

## Finding M — paper § 7.2.5 wording 의 두 부정확

(a) Pierce-Schott 2020 에서 prescription opioid 가 "primary causal mediator" 로 specify 안 됨 — drug overdose 가 outcome, labor market 이 mechanism.

두 reference 를 "opioid-pathway mechanism ... prescription opioids served as a primary causal mediator" 로 cumulative 묶은 wording 이 두 source 의 substantive 영역을 conflate.
