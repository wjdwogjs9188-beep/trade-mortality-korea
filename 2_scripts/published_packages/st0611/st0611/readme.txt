The dataset is available from this site.

To download the community-contributed Stata ado-files and do-files associated
with the Stata Journal article, type the following commands in Stata:

. net from http://www.stata-journal.com/software/sj20-3/
. net describe st0611
. net install st0611
. net get st0611

or you could

 1. select Help and then SJ and community-contributed commands
 2. click on Stata Journal
 3. click on sj20-3
 4. click on st0611
 5. click on "install"
 5. click on "click here to get"

Note that the do-files and other ancillary files will be copied to your
current directory.
