# [#25] Does Job Loss Shorten Life?

## 메타정보
- **저자**: Marcus Eliason (Göteborg/IFAU), Donald Storrie (European Foundation)
- **출판년도**: 2009
- **학술지**: **Journal of Human Resources** vol 44(2): 277-302
- **JEL**: J63, J65, I12

## Research Question
스웨덴 plant closure (1987-1988) 로 인한 job loss 가 **all-cause + cause-specific mortality** 에 미치는 인과 영향.

## Data
- Swedish linked employer-employee data (LISA)
- 13,943 displaced workers (25-64세, 760 establishment closures, 1987-1988)
- Control: 146,687 nondisplaced workers (random sample)
- Mortality data 1987-2003 (Swedish death registry, ICD-8/9/10)

## Identification
**Plant closure as exogenous shock** (모든 worker 실직):
- Selection bias 통제: closure 는 individual health-irrelevant
- Propensity score matching + Cox proportional hazards
- Cause-specific: cancer, alcohol-related, suicide

## Main Result
**Male displaced workers (4년 단기):**
- All-cause mortality risk **+44%** (vs nondisplaced)
- Suicide: **2배 (twofold) increase**
- Alcohol-related: **2배 increase**
- Cancer: no significant change

**Female displaced workers:**
- All-cause: no significant change
- Suicide·alcohol: 약 2배 (작은 sample)
- Long-run (after 4 years): no persistent effect

## Connection to Trade × Mortality Korea
**역할: MEDIATOR ANCHOR — job loss → mortality channel (Sweden = small open economy analog)**

- 스웨덴 = 작은 open economy (Korea 와 비슷)
- Plant closure = displacement-as-shock 의 cleanest 형태 (selection 통제)
- Cause-specific mortality (suicide, alcohol) 가 Case-Deaton deaths of despair 와 직접 매핑

## 본 paper 와의 connection
- Trade shock → manufacturing employment ↓ → displacement → mortality 의 channel 의 micro foundation
- 본 paper 의 sigungu mortality 변화 mechanism 의 anchor
- 한국 노동시장의 displacement 효과 추정에 직접 활용 가능 (1997 IMF 위기 시 plant closure 가 자연실험)
