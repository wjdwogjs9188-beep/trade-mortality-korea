# [#26] Job Displacement and Mortality: An Analysis Using Administrative Data

## 메타정보
- **저자**: Daniel Sullivan (Chicago Fed), Till von Wachter (Columbia)
- **출판년도**: 2009
- **학술지**: **Quarterly Journal of Economics** vol 124(3): 1265-1306
- **DOI**: 10.1162/qjec.2009.124.3.1265

## Research Question
미국 manufacturing displaced worker 의 **mortality hazard** 변화. Earnings loss 와의 correlation 검증 (selection vs causation 분리).

## Data
- Pennsylvania quarterly UI records (1970s-1980s) → SSA death certificates (1980-2006)
- High-tenure male workers (≥6년 또는 ≥3년)
- Mass layoff/plant closure 시 firms

## Identification
- **Mass layoff sample**: 산업·기업 condition 통제, 개인 selection 통제
- Mean / variance earnings 통제
- Workers with similar earnings trajectories matched

## Main Result
**Short-run (first year post-displacement):**
- Mortality hazard **+50% to +100%** vs counterfactual
- Effect sharply declines over time

**Long-run (20 years post-displacement):**
- 10-15% increase in annual death hazard PERSISTS
- → **Life expectancy loss: 1.0-1.5 years** for worker displaced at age 40

**Heterogeneity:**
- Bigger earnings loss → bigger mortality increase
- Industry / firm / firm-size wage premium loss → mortality 으로 transmit
- Workers near retirement (~62세): little effect (selection out)

- Earnings loss → mortality transmission 의 magnitude provides priors

## 본 paper § 9 mechanism 인용 안
> "Sullivan and von Wachter (2009) document that high-tenure male workers displaced from 
> Pennsylvania manufacturing experience a 50-100% increase in short-run mortality hazard and 
> a persistent 10-15% increase even 20 years later, implying a life expectancy loss of 1.0-1.5 
> years for workers displaced at age 40. Our sigungu-level estimates capture aggregate analogues 
> of this individual-level mortality effect, providing the first such evidence for Korea."

## 본 paper § 5 spec 비교
- Sullivan-vW 는 individual hazard model. 본 paper 는 sigungu-level Δlog mortality
- 둘 다 ~10-15% magnitude 가 long-run 에 안정적
- 본 paper 의 β=−0.069 (Phase 4 publishable, 5-year diff Δlog) 와 magnitude 비교 가능
